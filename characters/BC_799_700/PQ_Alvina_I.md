# Alvina I (815 BC - 752 BC)
Alvina was the Queen of Foundry Cove, Electress of Foundry Cove and Baroness of the Foundry. She was born in 815 BC to the King and Queen Aedan III and Sarai, and she eventually passed away in 752 BC at the age of 63.

## Spouses

### Peregrinus (804 BC - ?)
Peregrinus "Shawn" was born in 804 BC. He and Alvina eventually married in 793 BC.

## Reign (809 BC - 769 BC)
Alvina ascended to the throne of Foundry Cove upon the death of her father, Aedan. Alvina reigned during a time when the Nobles and Barons of Foundry insituted legal frameworks to limit the reigns of monarchs. With the legal provisions, Alvina was to only reign until her eldest son reaches the age of 15. Alvina however reigned until Hypnos was aged 23.

### 2nd Reign (767 BC - 757 BC)
Hypnos however passed away in 767 BC at the age of 25, and the nobles decided to vote unamoiously for Alvina's return. The nobles noted that Alvina's reign was to expire when Hypnos' son Romanus reaches the age of 15, or when she passes. Alvina ended her reign in 757 BC at the age of 58, and retired from her royal duties.
